The Beginning

The first breath underwater changes something.

The first breath underwater changes something. I did my first try dive in Egypt at 13, and by the time I came back up I already wanted to go back down. A few years later, a try dive in the Maldives in 2022 ended with a shark encounter that settled it for good.

I got my recent PADI Open Water in February 2026 through Bermuda Diving Center, Dubai. My first two logged dives were in Khor Fakkan with Dive Campus, the same centre where I did the buoyancy workshop. The plan from here is straightforward: Advanced Open Water, night diving, and eventually a Rescue Diver certification.
